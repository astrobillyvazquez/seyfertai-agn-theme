Eye-strain strategy: the Soft Contrast variant reduces the two biggest sources of dark-theme fatigue — extreme luminance contrast between pure-black backgrounds and pure-white text, and over-saturated accent colors. The ground is lifted to #141C30 (grey-indigo), foreground is warmed to #DDE2ED (not pure white), and every accent is pulled ~15% off its base chroma. AGN-gold becomes warmer amber, plasma pink softens toward rose, cyan toward sky, but hue identity is preserved so the theme still reads as Seyfert AGN.

Scrollbar visibility: the thumb uses indigo-light at 72% opacity (base theme used 14%). On hover it lifts to 88%. When dragging it flips to full-opacity AGN-gold — the sacred color surfaces at the moment of active interaction. Same gesture as a modified-file indicator.
